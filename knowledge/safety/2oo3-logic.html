2oo3 Voting Logic

📅 Jan 2025🏷️ SIS, Safety

The Hook

At NEOM Green Hydrogen, a single sensor failure triggered a $2M emergency shutdown. The fix? 2oo3 voting logic requiring 2 of 3 sensors to agree.

Core Concept

  • Fault Tolerance: Requires 2 sensors to agree, preventing false trips
  • SIL Compliance: Meets SIL2/SIL3 requirements
  • Voting Types: 1oo2, 2oo3, and 2oo4 configurations
  • Alarm Priority: LL→HH→L→H→fault→normal state machine
  • NAMUR NE43: Failure indicated at 3.8-21.2mA range

Real-World Story

During NEOM reactor commissioning, temperature sensor drift caused false trip. Investigation revealed single sensor voting. Installing 2oo3 logic prevented further unplanned shutdowns.

Takeaway: 2oo3 requires 2 of 3 sensors to agree, providing fault-tolerant safety trips.

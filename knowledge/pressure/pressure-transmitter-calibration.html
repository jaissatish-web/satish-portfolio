Pressure Transmitter Calibration

📅 Jan 2025🏷️ Pressure, Calibration

The Hook

Pressure transmitter reading 5% high during NSRP pre-commissioning. Five-point calibration revealed zero shift — corrected in 15 minutes.

Core Concept

  • 5-Point Calibration: 0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100% span points
  • Zero Adjustment: Set 4mA at no applied pressure
  • Span Adjustment: Set 20mA at full scale pressure
  • Error Formula: %error = (Actual - Expected) / Span × 100
  • Hysteresis: Check rising vs falling curves

Interactive Simulator

Real-World Story

At ADNOC Habshan, pressure transmitter PIC-201 showed 5% high reading. Investigation found zero at 4.2mA instead of 4.0mA. Correction prevented potential relief valve setpoint errors.

Takeaway: Always perform 5-point calibration — zero shifts are common causes of error.
